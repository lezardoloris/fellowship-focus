## Detailed description
Fellowship Focus blocks the sites that eat your day and keeps you in deep work.

WHAT IT DOES
• Instant block — a site you added is stopped the moment you navigate to it, before the page loads.
• Already-open tabs die too — turn the shield on and open distractions are redirected right away.
• Deep Work mode — block only Shorts, Reels and feeds while tutorials and DMs still work.
• Two block styles — a full block page with a focus quote, or a quiet "-XP" notification that bounces the tab back.
• Focus timer — Pomodoro-style sessions with your chosen work/break rhythm.
• Optional guild — connect a Fellowship to bet focus with friends: XP, a weekly ladder, and accountability when you cave.

PRIVACY FIRST
Everything is stored on your device. Nothing about your browsing is sold, shared, or uploaded. The only data that ever leaves your browser is the block/session events you send to the guild you choose to connect — and if you never connect one, nothing is sent at all.

Pairs with the Fellowship Focus desktop app, which blocks across every browser and app system-wide.

## Single purpose
Block distracting websites during focus sessions.
